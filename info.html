Current status of the Kazakh language

Kazakh language is the official language of Kazakhstan. It is used in government agencies, educational institutions and the media.

The role of the Kazakh language in various spheres of life

The Kazakh language is used in literature, art, cinema, music, science, economics and other fields. An example is the work of the Kazakh writer Mukhtar Auezov “The Great Silk Road”, which has become a classic of world literature.

Problems associated with the preservation and development of the Kazakh language

There are problems, such as a decrease in the level of knowledge of the Kazakh language among younger generations and the threat of its replacement by other languages. There is also a lack of educational and information resources in the Kazakh language.

Measures taken to preserve and develop the Kazakh language

To preserve and develop the Kazakh language, language campaigns are carried out, government programs are developed and implemented, educational materials and media in the Kazakh language are created, and Kazakh-language culture is supported and developed.
